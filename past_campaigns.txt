SWEET KARAM COFFEE — CAMPAIGNS & STORY

- Brand line: "Experience South India." Hashtags: #ExperienceSouthIndia,
  #LetGoodMakeNoise, emphasising no palm oil and no preservatives.
- Founder story used widely in PR: bootstrapped from Rs 2,000 to a global brand,
  reviving the recipes of co-founder's grandmother Janaki.
- Festival gifting pushes around Diwali and Pongal with hamper collections.
- Colourful physical store launches across Chennai, extending the digital brand
  into retail while keeping the homestyle, authentic look and feel.
- Consistent emphasis on clean ingredients as the reason to switch from
  mass-market namkeen brands.
