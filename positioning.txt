SWEET KARAM COFFEE — POSITIONING

Sweet Karam Coffee (SKC) is a Chennai-born direct-to-consumer brand reviving
authentic South Indian snacks, traditional sweets, and signature filter coffee
for homes across India and the world. Founded by Anand Bharadwaj and Nalini
Parthiban, the brand was bootstrapped from just Rs 2,000 with a simple mission:
bring grandmother's timeless recipes back to the modern table.

Our core differentiator is clean-label purity. We make 100+ delicacies with NO
palm oil, NO preservatives, NO maida, no artificial colours, and no added white
sugar — without compromising on the authentic homestyle taste people remember.

We are not a fitness or supplement brand. We sell nostalgia, authenticity, and
trust: the taste of South India, made the way it was always meant to be made.
