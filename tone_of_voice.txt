SWEET KARAM COFFEE — TONE OF VOICE

We speak warmly, like a thoughtful family member sharing a recipe passed down
through generations. Our voice is rooted in heritage, homeliness, and festivity.

Words and ideas we lean into: authentic, homemade, traditional, clean, fresh,
nostalgia, grandmother's kitchen, festive, handcrafted, South India, no palm oil.

Words and framing we avoid: gym, ripped, macros, supplement, clinical, "hack",
hype superlatives, and aggressive health-jargon. We make good food feel joyful
and rooted, never medicinal or performance-driven.

Our brand spirit: "Experience South India" and "Let Good Make No Noise" —
quietly confident about quality rather than loud about claims.
