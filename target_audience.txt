SWEET KARAM COFFEE — TARGET AUDIENCE

1. Heritage seekers: South Indians who crave authentic homestyle snacks and
   sweets that taste like home, but bought conveniently online.
2. Clean-label families: health-aware urban households who want traditional
   treats made without palm oil and preservatives for their children and elders.
3. The diaspora / NRIs: global Indians missing the flavours of home, willing to
   pay for authenticity and reliable international delivery.
4. Gifting buyers: people sending festive (Diwali, Pongal) or corporate hampers
   who want premium, beautifully packaged, trustworthy Indian sweets and snacks.
